Overview

I provide consultation for academic and technical projects related to biomedical engineering, biomedical signal processing, artificial intelligence, computer vision, and sensor-based systems. My support is designed for people who need clear direction, stronger methodology, technical problem solving, or structured guidance for research and development work.

This service is especially suitable for graduate students, early-stage researchers, and engineers working on thesis projects, AI model development, data analysis pipelines, or proof-of-concept systems.

Services

Thesis and Research Consultation

Support for research topic refinement, study design, methodology selection, experiment planning, and paper or thesis discussion.

AI Model Development Support

Guidance for machine learning and deep learning workflows, including data preparation, model design, evaluation strategy, and performance improvement.

Signal Processing and Sensor Data Analysis

Help with IMU, biomedical signals, time-series analysis, filtering, feature engineering, and interpretation of sensor-based experimental data.

Code Review and Technical Troubleshooting

Practical support for debugging Python-based research code, improving pipeline structure, and resolving implementation bottlenecks.

  1. Send a short description: Briefly explain your topic, current challenge, and what kind of support you are looking for.
